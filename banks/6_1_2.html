2.1
A light ray strikes a flat mirror. Which measurement correctly describes the angle of incidence?
A) The angle between the incoming ray and the mirror surface
B) The angle between the incoming ray and the normal to the mirror surface
C) The angle between the incoming ray and the reflected ray
D) The angle between the normal and the mirror surface
E) The angle between the reflected ray and the mirror surface

B) 30° from the mirror surface

E) 90° from the normal

E) They add up to 90°

B) The reflected ray rotates 20°
