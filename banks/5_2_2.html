B) They consist of oscillating electric and magnetic fields
C) They transfer matter

C) Mechanical longitudinal
D) Electromagnetic

A) Matter transport
B) Transverse wave motion

A) Sound frequency too low
B) No medium in space
C) Sound speed too slow
D) Waves become transverse

C) Greater amplitude

A) It remains the same
B) It depends on medium properties
C) It becomes zero

A) It disappears completely
B) It reflects back along the string
C) It permanently moves the wall

A) It increases
B) It decreases
C) It remains unchanged
D) It becomes zero
