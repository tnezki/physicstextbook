Formula 1: Frequency–Period Relationship
Formula: $$f = \frac{1}{T}$$ Variables: f (Hz), T (s)

Answer: __________ Hz
3.2

Answer: __________ s
3.3

Answer: __________ s
3.4

Answer: __________ Hz
Formula 2: Wave Equation
Formula: $$v = f\lambda$$ Variables: v (m/s), f (Hz), λ (m)
3.5

Answer: __________ m/s
3.6
Find the wavelength of a wave traveling at 18 m/s with frequency 6.0 Hz.
Answer: __________ m
3.7
A water wave moves at 3.5 m/s and has wavelength 0.70 m. What is its frequency?
Answer: __________ Hz
3.8
Determine the wave speed of a 4.0 Hz wave with wavelength 1.5 m.
Answer: __________ m/s
