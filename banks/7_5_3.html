600 W

5.0 A
$$I = \frac{P}{V} = \frac{600}{120} = 5.0\text{ A}$$

12.5 A
$$I = \frac{1500}{120} = 12.5\text{ A}$$
3.4
50 V
$$V = \frac{P}{I} = \frac{200}{4.0} = 50\text{ V}$$

3.5
72 W
$$P = I^2R = (3.0)^2(8) = 72\text{ W}$$

48 W

3.7
80 W
$$P = (4.0)^2(5) = 80\text{ W}$$
3.8
3.0 A
$$I = \sqrt{\frac{P}{R}} = \sqrt{\frac{90}{10}} = 3.0\text{ A}$$

3.9
600 W
$$P = \frac{120^2}{24} = 600\text{ W}$$

1920 W
$$P = \frac{240^2}{30} = 1920\text{ W}$$

50 Ω
$$R = \frac{V^2}{P} = \frac{200^2}{800} = 50\text{ Ω}$$
