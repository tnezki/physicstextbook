A) The battery maintains a potential difference
B) The drift velocity is extremely high
C) Resistance increases over time
D) The charges are created continuously
E) The electrons move at the speed of light

B) Neutrons
C) Free electrons
D) Ions

B) The meter may be damaged due to low resistance path
C) The voltage increases

E) The drift velocity becomes zero

B) Charge
C) Voltage
D) Power
E) Mass

B) Current gradually increases
C) Current stops because no energy source remains

B) The available electrical energy decreases
C) The electrons stop moving permanently

E) The ammeter reverses direction

C) Eliminate voltage
