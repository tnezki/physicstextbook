Formula: Critical Angle
Formula: $\sin\theta_c = \dfrac{n_2}{n_1}$    or equivalently    $\theta_c = \sin^{-1}\!\left(\dfrac{n_2}{n_1}\right)$

Variables: $\theta_c$ = critical angle (degrees), $n_1$ = refractive index of the denser medium (light source side), $n_2$ = refractive index of the less dense medium

Condition: $n_1 > n_2$ (light travels from denser medium toward less dense medium)
