Single-Step Calculations

Formula Group 1 of 2
Formula 1: Electric Current
$$I = \frac{Q}{t}$$
3.1
A total charge of 12 C passes through a wire in 3.0 s. What is the current?
Answer: __________ A
3.2
Calculate the charge that flows in 4.0 s if the current is 2.5 A.
Answer: __________ C
3.3
A battery-powered device transfers 18 C of charge in 6.0 s while operating. What current does the device draw?
Answer: __________ A
3.4
Determine the time required for 30 C of charge to pass through a circuit if the current is 5.0 A.
Answer: __________ s
Formula 2: Electrical Energy
$$E = QV$$
3.5
A charge of 6.0 C moves across a potential difference of 9.0 V. How much electrical energy is transferred?
Answer: __________ J
3.6
Calculate the charge moved if 45 J of energy is transferred across a 15 V source.
Answer: __________ C
3.7
A rechargeable battery transfers 120 J of energy to a circuit operating at 12 V. What total charge moves through the circuit?
Answer: __________ C
3.8
A 4.0 C charge passes through a device and 80 J of electrical energy is delivered. What was the potential difference?
Answer: __________ V
