Questions 4.1 – 4.4: Refractive Index and Light Speed
4.1
$n_B / n_A = 4/3 \approx 1.33$ | $n_A = 3.0/2.4 = 1.25$; $n_B = 3.0/1.8 = 1.67$; ratio $= 1.67/1.25 = 1.33$
4.2
$v = 1.24 \times 10^8$ m/s; 2.42 times slower | $v = c/n = 3.0 \times 10^8 / 2.42 \approx 1.24 \times 10^8$ m/s; ratio $= 2.42/1.00 = 2.42$
4.3
$n = 1.50$ | $v = d/t = 1.2/(6.0 \times 10^{-9}) = 2.0 \times 10^8$ m/s; $n = c/v = 3.0 \times 10^8 / 2.0 \times 10^8 = 1.50$
4.4
Speed difference $= 0.833 \times 10^8$ m/s (or $8.33 \times 10^7$ m/s) | $v_1 = 3.0/1.20 = 2.50 \times 10^8$; $v_2 = 3.0/1.80 = 1.67 \times 10^8$; diff $= 0.833 \times 10^8$ m/s
Questions 4.5 – 4.8: Snell's Law — Angles and Multiple Media
4.5
Angle in water $\approx 43.8°$ | Step 1: $(1.00)\sin 50° = (1.50)\sin\theta_{glass}$; $\theta_{glass} = \sin^{-1}(0.766/1.50) = \sin^{-1}(0.511) \approx 30.7°$. Step 2: $(1.50)\sin 30.7° = (1.33)\sin\theta_w$; $\sin\theta_w = (1.50 \times 0.511)/1.33 = 0.577$; $\theta_w = \sin^{-1}(0.577) \approx 35.3°$
4.6
Angle in air $\approx 34.2°$ | $(1.00)\sin\theta_1 = (1.33)\sin 25.0°$; $\sin\theta_1 = 1.33 \times 0.4226 = 0.5620$; $\theta_1 = \sin^{-1}(0.5620) \approx 34.2°$
4.7
Angle in glass $\approx 22.7°$ | Step 1 (air→glycerol): $(1.00)\sin 35.0° = (1.47)\sin\theta_g$; $\sin\theta_g = 0.5736/1.47 = 0.3902$; $\theta_g \approx 22.97°$. Step 2 (glycerol→glass): $(1.47)\sin 22.97° = (1.52)\sin\theta_{glass}$; $\sin\theta_{glass} = (1.47 \times 0.3902)/1.52 = 0.3773$; $\theta_{glass} \approx 22.1°$
4.8
Difference $\approx 7.8°$ | Block A: $\sin\theta_A = (1.00)\sin 45°/1.50 = 0.4714$; $\theta_A \approx 28.1°$. Block B: $\sin\theta_B = 0.7071/1.80 = 0.3928$; $\theta_B \approx 23.1°$. Difference $= 28.1° - 23.1° = 5.0°$ *(Accept 4.9° – 5.1° depending on rounding)*
Questions 4.9 – 4.12: Real-World Combined Applications
4.9
$n \approx 2.21$; $\theta_2 \approx 16.7°$ | $n = c/v = 3.0 \times 10^8 / 1.36 \times 10^8 \approx 2.21$; $(1.00)\sin 40° = (2.21)\sin\theta_2$; $\sin\theta_2 = 0.6428/2.21 = 0.2909$; $\theta_2 \approx 16.9°$
4.10
Angle in water $\approx 40.6°$; deflection $\approx 19.4°$ | $(1.00)\sin 60° = (1.33)\sin\theta_w$; $\sin\theta_w = 0.8660/1.33 = 0.651$; $\theta_w \approx 40.6°$; deflection $= 60.0° - 40.6° = 19.4°$
4.11
$n \approx 1.55$; $v \approx 1.94 \times 10^8$ m/s | $(1.00)\sin 55° = n\sin 32°$; $n = 0.8192/0.5299 \approx 1.546 \approx 1.55$; $v = 3.0 \times 10^8/1.55 \approx 1.94 \times 10^8$ m/s
4.12
Intermediate angle $\approx 33.7°$; final angle $= 48.0°$ (confirms reversibility) | Step 1 (air→water): $(1.00)\sin 48° = (1.33)\sin\theta_w$; $\sin\theta_w = 0.7431/1.33 = 0.5587$; $\theta_w \approx 33.97° \approx 34.0°$. Step 2 (water→air): $(1.33)\sin 34.0° = (1.00)\sin\theta_{air}$; $\sin\theta_{air} = 1.33 \times 0.5592 = 0.7437$; $\theta_{air} = \sin^{-1}(0.7437) \approx 48.0°$ ✓
