180 m/s
$\lambda = \frac{2(0.90)}{4} = 0.450$ m $v = f\lambda = 400(0.450) = 180$ m/s

600 m/s
$\lambda = \frac{2(2.0)}{2} = 2.0$ m $v = f\lambda = 150(2.0) = 300$ m/s Correction: $\lambda = 2.0$ m $v = 150 × 2.0 = 300$ m/s Final: 300 m/s

40.0 Hz
