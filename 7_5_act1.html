Vocabulary Mixer — Find Your Set

Goal: Build complete vocabulary sets by matching terms, meanings, units, formulas, and examples.

  • Each person gets one card.
  • Walk around and find the other four cards that belong with yours.
  • When you think your set is correct, sit together.
  • Be ready to explain why your five cards belong together.
  • After your set is formed, complete your challenge question.

Then your group will share out:

  • What your concept is
  • How the cards connect
  • One common mistake students make
  • The answer to your challenge question

Rounds

  • Round 1: Sets 1–8
  • Round 2: Sets 9–15

Your teacher will print the cut-out cards separately.

Check Your Understanding

1. Electrical power is best described as:
A) total energy used
B) resistance to charge flow
C) rate of energy transfer
D) charge per second
2. In a parallel circuit, each branch has the same:
A) current
B) voltage
C) resistance
D) power
3. Which equation represents electrical energy?
A) $V = IR$
B) $P = IV$
C) $E = Pt$
D) $I = \frac{Q}{t}$
4. A conductor is a material that:
A) has very high resistance
B) blocks charge flow
C) allows charge to flow easily
D) measures current
5. If resistance increases while voltage stays constant, current will:
A) increase
B) decrease
C) stay the same
D) reverse direction
6. Voltage is best defined as:
A) energy per charge
B) charge per second
C) opposition to current
D) total energy used
7. Current is measured in:
A) volts
B) watts
C) amperes
D) ohms
8. A series circuit is characterized by:
A) multiple paths
B) one path for current
C) equal voltage across all branches
D) zero total resistance
9. Which device is connected in series to measure current?
A) voltmeter
B) solar cell
C) ammeter
D) resistor
10. A solar cell is best described as a device that:
A) stores electrical charge
B) converts light to electrical energy
C) measures voltage
D) blocks current flow
