Physics Showdown

Section 7.1: Electric Charge and Electric Force

Showdown Directions
Setup: Groups of 4 • each student needs a whiteboard • assign a Captain each round.

1. Read & Think (30s): The Captain reads the problem aloud. Everyone gets silent think time.

2. Work Alone: All 4 teammates solve silently on their own. No talking or peeking. When done, flip your board face-down and give thumbs up.

3. SHOWDOWN! When everyone is ready, the Captain calls “SHOWDOWN!” and all answers are revealed at the same time.
4. Check & Celebrate: If all are correct, celebrate. If answers differ, discuss, find the mistake, teach each other, then celebrate.

5. Rotate Captain: The person to the left becomes the new Captain.

Good teamwork:
  • “Let me show you how I did that...”
  • “Oh, I see where I made my mistake!”
  • “What method did you use?”
Avoid:
  • Showing work before “SHOWDOWN!”
  • Rushing teammates
  • Copying someone else’s answer
